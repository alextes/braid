# research findings template

copy this structure into the issue body under a `## research findings` heading.

```markdown
## research findings

### context

<!-- what area of the codebase did you explore? what are the key observations? -->

### options considered

#### option A: <name>

- **approach:** <what would this look like?>
- **pros:** <benefits>
- **cons:** <drawbacks>
- **effort:** <small / medium / large>
- **risk:** <low / medium / high — what could go wrong?>

#### option B: <name>

- **approach:** <what would this look like?>
- **pros:** <benefits>
- **cons:** <drawbacks>
- **effort:** <small / medium / large>
- **risk:** <low / medium / high — what could go wrong?>

### open questions

<!-- things you couldn't determine, need human input on, or that affect the choice -->

- <question 1>
- <question 2>

### recommendation

<!-- which option do you lean toward and why? frame as a suggestion, not a decision. -->
```
